Why Should You Use Chatbot?

When we think about why Chatbot should be used, we see that the benefits to companies and customers are more than one. Chatbots can instantly bring us the information or product we are looking for on a website while chatting, and can instantly perform transactions with live support or a call center. Companies offer chatbots that provide information to their customers, carry out various operations, and provide product benefits that can offer many different benefits. Chatbots can be used proactively or reactively. They work reactively only when they develop to respond to their customers' communication requests. The customer does not make any communication without asking questions. When they are developed proactively, they automatically communicate with customers in line with the goals and scenarios previously determined. For example, when the product that the customer wants to buy falls below a certain price, the party that communicates with it may be chatbots.
